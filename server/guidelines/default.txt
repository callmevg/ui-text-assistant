This file contains the default writing style guidelines in text format.

Use clear and concise language.
Avoid jargon and technical terms unless necessary.
Maintain a friendly and approachable tone.
Use active voice whenever possible.
Be consistent with terminology and formatting.
Break up large blocks of text for better readability.
Use bullet points or numbered lists for clarity.
Ensure proper grammar and punctuation.
Tailor the content to the target audience's level of understanding.
Provide examples where applicable to illustrate points.
